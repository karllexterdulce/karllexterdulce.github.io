TAHO

Ang tulang tungkol sa nakaraang kasintamis ng arnibal at kasinglambot ng malasutlang tokwa — ang init ng yakap na nagmumula sa taglimang pisong taho.
A poem about a past as sweet as syrup and as soft as silken tofu — the warmth of an embrace poured from a five-peso cup of taho.
